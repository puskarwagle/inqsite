# Quest Bot - Your AI-Powered Job Search Team

Quest Bot is a suite of intelligent AI agents that work together to automate and accelerate your job search. Think of it as having a dedicated team of specialists working 24/7 to land your dream job.

---

## Meet Your AI Agents

### Job Hunter Agent
Your tireless job search companion that scours multiple job boards simultaneously.

**Supported Platforms:**
- Seek (Australia)
- LinkedIn
- Indeed

**What it does:**
- Searches jobs matching your criteria across all platforms
- Filters out irrelevant listings automatically
- Tracks which jobs you've applied to
- Manages pagination to find hidden opportunities
- Works while you sleep - queue up searches and let it run

---

### Application Agent
The workhorse that handles the tedious application process for you.

**Capabilities:**
- Fills out application forms automatically
- Uploads your resume to each application
- Handles multi-step application flows
- Navigates "Easy Apply" and quick apply features
- Submits applications with human-like timing and behavior

**Smart Features:**
- Anti-detection technology to avoid bot blockers
- Session persistence - stay logged in across runs
- Real-time progress overlay so you can monitor activity
- Graceful error handling and automatic retries

---

### Cover Letter Agent
Crafts personalized, compelling cover letters in seconds.

**How it works:**
1. Analyzes the job description
2. Matches your experience to job requirements
3. Generates a tailored cover letter highlighting relevant skills
4. Maintains your authentic voice and style

**Benefits:**
- No more staring at blank pages
- Each letter is unique to the position
- Highlights the right experience for each role
- ATS-optimized formatting

---

### Resume Builder Agent
Creates professional, ATS-friendly resumes that get past the bots and impress humans.

**Features:**
- Multiple professional templates
- Single and two-column layouts
- Customizable fonts and styling
- Real-time preview
- DOCX export for easy editing

**Sections supported:**
- Professional summary
- Work experience with bullet points
- Education history
- Skills (categorized)
- Certifications
- Custom sections

---

### Q&A Agent
Intelligently answers employer-specific application questions.

**Handles questions like:**
- "Why do you want to work here?"
- "Describe your experience with [technology]"
- "What's your expected salary?"
- Years of experience questions
- Availability and start date
- Work authorization

**Intelligence:**
- Learns from your profile and past answers
- Maintains consistency across applications
- Adapts tone to match company culture

## How It Works

1. **Set Up Your Profile** - Enter your experience, skills, and job preferences once

3. **Launch Your Search** - Let the agents work while you focus on prep

5. **Land Interviews** - Receive notifications when employers respond
